Cars.com is a leading digital marketplace and solutions provider for the automotive industry that connects car shoppers with sellers. Car Shoppers Put Pedal to the Metal Labor Day Weekend: 51% Ready to Buy, Even More Hit The Road for Holiday Travel, According to Cars.com

Sedans Return: 41% of Labor Day Shoppers Are Looking for a Reliable Style

CHICAGO, Aug. 31, 2020 /PRNewswire/ -- The latest research from Cars.com (NYSE: CARS), a leading digital automotive marketplace and solutions provider, found that 60% of Americans plan to travel this Labor Day weekend,1 nearly double the amount compared to Memorial Day (34%),2 and 88% are planning to drive. As automobiles continue to be the primary mode of transportation during the COVID-19 pandemic, Cars.com's research found virus concerns are motivating more than 50% of in-market shoppers to buy a car this holiday weekend — much sooner than originally planned.1
